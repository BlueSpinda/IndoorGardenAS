Why Indoor Gardening My Experience

Plants do not grow overnight, but they are always growing, little by little. And the coolest thing about them is that every once in a while, they grow enough such that their change becomes noticeable to us, waving at you gently and quietly to say, "look, I am growing everyday. Thank you for taking care of me." I still remember the first time seeing my spider plant flower. The delicate little white flower that grew out at the end of a thin stem. The joy of nurturing and witnessing growth of my plants soothed and pampered me, led me to the wonders of the plant world. And I hope to take you there.
